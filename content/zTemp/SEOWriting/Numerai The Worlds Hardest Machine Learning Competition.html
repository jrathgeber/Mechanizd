The History and Evolution of Numerai

In 2015, Richard Craib, a mathematician, started Numerai. He moved from traditional finance to a new, decentralized model. The platform began by using collective intelligence. But it really took off when it added cryptocurrency to its core.

In 2016, Numerai launched Numeraire, its own token. It paid data scientists for their work. This brought blockchain into its system, linking rewards to cryptocurrency principles. By 2018, it became even more open, welcoming people from all over.
